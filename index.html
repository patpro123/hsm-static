Welcome to the world of MUSIC Let our Experts be your guide

In the heart of the twin city of the Deccan, we have set up a place where the inner musician in everyone can be nurtured. With a pledge to turn each student a musician, our passionate faculty has created goal driven, well structured courses in guitar, keyboard , percussion and vocal music. Come and experience the journey with us - the journey from sound to music.

Our mission is to deliver access to the benefits of high-quality, tuition-free music education. Through intensive instruction and performance, our students achieve excellence in music that transfers to other areas in life. They grow musically, socially, emotionally and intellectually, and develop a foundation of responsibility, self-esteem, resilience and purpose.

At Hyderabad School of Music , we envision a society where all children have access to the unique social, emotional, intellectual benefits achieved through musical excellence. We believe music is transformative and empowers the lives of youth, families and communities.

We believe in hard work and equal recognition for all. We nurture creativity in all, and embody community through our diverse student and faculty base.
